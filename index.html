Hello! I am a master's student at UC Berkeley studying Information Management and Systems. In the past, I have co-founded and led the operations of an auto-detailing products manufacturing company. I have been an MIT and IIT Kanpur recognized Entrepreneur. I have also served as a research assistant to Dr. Serena Chen in the Department of Psychology at UC Berkeley for three years. I enjoy bringing together insights from a variety of disciplines to solve complex problems. My current goal is to design commercial technology products that will facilitate social and emotional learning on a global scale. In my free time, I like to travel and read non-fiction.
